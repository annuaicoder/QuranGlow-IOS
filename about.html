About QuranGlow

The Project Lead, Site Administrator, and Founder of QuranGlow is the open-source developer Anas (@annuaicoder on GitHub and @codewithanas007 on YouTube).

Our Mission

QuranGlow was created with a single purpose: to provide a peaceful, distraction-free space for reading the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed. Every feature — from color-coded Tajweed rules to an ad-free interface — is designed to help readers focus on the words of Allah without interruptions. Whether you’re reciting for study, reflection, or memorization, QuranGlow offers a clean and calm environment for your journey.
